The British Invasion of Tibet

I created this map by tracing the route of the British conquest in Tibet through Sikkim, India. This event was significant to me as I have traveled through the Tibetan region and wanted to learn more about this often forgotten conquest. The basemap is a digital elevation model of the Himalayas with the red dots showing battles and other significant events along the route.
