Mapbox Psychedelic

(An experiment in aesthetics)

This map was made for an assignment focused on aestheticism in mapping. We were given much creative freedom to emulate a certain style in our "slippy" map, referring to the capability of zooming and panning the map. I chose to recreate the style of the 1960's psychedelic movement with vivid colors and familiar icons to create a "trippy" feel.
